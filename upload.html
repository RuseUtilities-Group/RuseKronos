Upload School Timetable

How to upload your timetable from Sentral:

1. Login to Sentral

2. Go to the timetable page by clicking the navbar on the left side of the page.

3. Click "Export as iCal" in the top right corner.

4. Upload the downloaded file "my_timetable.ics" here.

5. Press submit to enter the RuseKronos WebApp
